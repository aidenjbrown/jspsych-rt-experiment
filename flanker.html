Flanker Task

This task takes about 5 minutes. After brief instructions and a short practice round, you'll see a series of five-letter strings (like "ffhff" or "hhhhh") and respond to the middle letter by pressing the F or H key. The task runs in fullscreen and ends with two short reflection questions.

This task records your responses and reaction times for research purposes. When you finish, your data is automatically saved to our research data repository. Continuing indicates your consent.
