Video Sequence Task

This short task shows a ~5 second video with three colors flashing briefly, one at a time. After it plays, you'll be asked which color appeared second.

This task records your response and reaction time for research purposes. When you finish, your data is automatically saved to our research data repository. Continuing indicates your consent.
