Response Time Tasks

This is a short test (~2 minutes) with two tasks:

  1. Reaction Time Task — Press Space when a dot appears on screen. 10 trials, about 20 seconds.
  2. Moving Target Task — Click a moving circle when it stops. 5 trials, about 90 seconds.

At the end you will see a results screen. Please copy the text from the box and send it to Aiden.
